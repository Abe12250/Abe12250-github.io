My Selected Data Science Book

I also selected a book from the free-data-science-books GitHub repository to learn more about the field.

Book Title: Social and Economic Networks: Models and Analysis

Author/Institute: Matthew O. Jackson (Stanford University)

URL: https://web.stanford.edu/~jacksonm/netbook.pdf

Why I am interested in this book: I am interested in this book because it demonstrates how human interactions and relationships can be studied scientifically through the lens of social and economic networks. Understanding these complex systems can provide valuable insights into human behavior, decision-making, and global connectivity.
